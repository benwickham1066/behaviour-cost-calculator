Scales your two-week observation to a full year. ×22 assumes the rate you observed holds across all 220 working days. Lower it if the fortnight you watched ran hotter than a normal one — ×11 assumes it holds for only half the year. SSC default: ×22.
The share of the estimated cost SSC expects to remove. SSC recodes the one keystone behaviour driving the pattern; the downstream behaviours quieten once it stops being triggered. This is an SSC modelling assumption — so it is yours to challenge. SSC default: 70%, deliberately conservative.
The share of your leaders who operate reactively — the population this cost is scaled across. This is not our figure to set: it is Leadership Circle's, measured across more than two million leaders. Lower it only if you have reason to believe your leadership runs below the global average. Default: 75% (Leadership Circle / Anderson & Adams).
What one stalled or avoided conversation costs you. The default is the conservative floor across five independent sources. If your own cost of a delayed decision is higher, set it here. SSC default: $1,500 at today's live rate.
