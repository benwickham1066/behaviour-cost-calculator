How to use. Observe for two working weeks. Each time you see a defensive behaviour stop a conversation, mark it in that day's column. Multiple marks per day is fine — this is not one tick per day, it is one mark per incident. Can't place it? Tick the nearest box. What matters is the flag, not the filing — the labels come into their own in a deeper diagnostic. At the end of two weeks, count each row and write the total in the Total column. Sum all rows for your grand total.
